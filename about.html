About ForgeTek

Practical Engineering.
Field-Ready Design.

ForgeTek Engineering Inc. is an Ontario-based electrical engineering consulting firm focused on industrial electrical systems, automation hardware, and field-supported design.

Built for Industrial Work

ForgeTek Engineering Inc. provides industrial electrical engineering support for facilities requiring practical, constructible, and reliable design solutions.

The company focuses on power distribution, motor control systems, control cabinets, industrial networks, and field engineering support for mining, forestry, manufacturing, and heavy industrial environments.

Our approach is straightforward: produce clear design packages, support construction, and solve real-world electrical problems with field conditions in mind.
