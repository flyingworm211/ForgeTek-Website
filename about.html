Engineering Built for the Field

ForgeTek Engineering Inc. is an independent electrical engineering consulting company serving mining, forestry, OSB mills, manufacturing facilities, and heavy industrial operations throughout Ontario.

The focus is simple: deliver practical, constructible designs that work in real operating environments. Every project is approached with an understanding of field installation, maintenance requirements, and long-term reliability.

Services include power distribution design, MCC and VFD systems, PLC hardware design, industrial networking, power factor correction studies, and commissioning support.
