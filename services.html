Engineering Services

ForgeTek Engineering Inc. provides practical industrial electrical engineering services for mining, forestry, manufacturing, and heavy industrial facilities.

Power Distribution & Electrical System Design

Electrical design for industrial power systems, including single-line diagrams, load calculations, transformer coordination, grounding, bonding, protection, cable sizing, cable tray layouts, and AutoCAD/Revit design drawings.

Motor Control & VFD Systems

MCC, VFD, soft starter, and motor control system design for conveyors, pumps, fans, ventilation systems, and process equipment. Services include motor control schematics, protection coordination, and integration support.

PLC & Industrial Automation Hardware

Design of PLC-based control systems, remote I/O systems, control cabinets, instrumentation wiring, panel layouts, and panel fabrication coordination.

Industrial Network Infrastructure

Industrial Ethernet, fiber optic, and plant network design for automation systems. Services include network topology drawings, switch layouts, cabinet design, and network cabinet panel fabrication coordination.

Power Factor Correction Bank Sizing

Power factor correction support including load review, single-line preparation, coordination with capacitor bank vendors, equipment sizing, and installation documentation.

Field Engineering & Commissioning

On-site engineering support during installation, startup, troubleshooting, contractor coordination, field verification, and commissioning of industrial electrical systems.
